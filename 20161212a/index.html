Why interactive/dynamic graphics?

  • Why interactive/dynamic graphics for presentation?
    • Aids communication in some way
      • Test assumptions, alter parameters, and/or explore details
  • Why interactive/dynamic graphics for exploration?
    • Generate insight faster (iteration time is crucial!!!).
      • Identify structure that might otherwise go missing (Tukey 1972)
      • Find Gestalt, pose queries, and make comparisons (Cook, Buja, & Swayne 2007)
      • Explore, understand, and diagnose statistical model fit(s) (Wickham, Cook, & Hofmann 2015).
  • Why web graphics?
    • simple to share, portable (web browser)
    • encourages composability

Web graphics usually aren't practical for exploring data

Good for presentation (viz type is known), bad for exploration (viz type is unknown)
